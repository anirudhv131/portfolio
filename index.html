Equity Research

The Cigna Group (NYSE: CI): A Healthcare Giant Mid-Transition

Cigna is the subject of conflicting analyst opinions, with one bank raising its price target to $400 and another cutting to $302 off the same set of facts. This report builds a discounted cash flow model off Cigna's segment-level financials and historical cash conversion, landing on a $319 price target, roughly 12% above the price at the time of writing. The thesis is that the market is treating a pharmacy-benefit pricing shift and two business divestitures as pure downside, when they are not.
